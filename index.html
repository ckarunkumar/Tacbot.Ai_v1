Progressive Autonomy

Human role shifts up the ladder: reviewer → approver of external side effects → workflow owner & exception handler.

L0

Manual

Organize cases & vendor interactions; users read and act.

L1

Assist

Summarize, explain alerts, draft responses, extract entities.

L2

Recommend

Suggest severity & next action; detect SLA risk; find gaps.

L3

Act w/ approval

Prepare escalation, draft updates, update fields — send after approval.

L4

Bounded automation

Auto-classify low-risk alerts; request diagnostics — with evals & rollback.

Autonomy is earned by evidence, not assumed from model capability.
