From signal to resolution in minutes.

Tacbot's four-stage pipeline turns raw vendor noise into confident, autonomous action — without pulling your team into triage hell.

Step 01
Ingest

Connect your vendor stack once. Tacbot pulls tickets, alerts, SLA data, and maintenance windows via API and webhooks — in real time.

Step 02
Analyze

The AI layer correlates signals, detects patterns, identifies conflicts, and scores each signal by urgency and business impact.

Step 03
Act

Low-risk actions — reschedules, acknowledgments, status updates — execute autonomously. High-stakes decisions get routed to the right human.

Step 04
Report

Every action is logged, every vendor scored, every SLA tracked. Export board-ready reports and use the data in your next contract renewal.
