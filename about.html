Built by leaders who have spent decades solving enterprise infrastructure challenges.

We founded the company after seeing the same problem repeated across thousands of enterprise support cases: organizations struggle to coordinate vendors, automate operations, and turn infrastructure events into business outcomes. The existing vendor engagement model has not changed in decades.

Why We Started

Throughout our careers, we watched highly skilled engineers spend countless hours manually correlating alerts, opening tickets, chasing vendors, and coordinating incidents.

We believed AI agents could do much more than answer questions — they could understand events, coordinate actions across systems, engage vendors automatically, and keep humans informed only when necessary.

Manual correlation
Autonomous orchestration

That belief became our company.
